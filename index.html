Hadith of the day

Will I See a Dream After Conducting the Istikhārah?
It is not necessary to see a dream, nor is it necessary to have a particular feeling. After the procedure of istikhārah, do what one feels is the best action, knowing that the best outcome is what the Almighty has decided. – (Al-Dur Al-Mukhtār, Qāmūs Al-Fiqh)
